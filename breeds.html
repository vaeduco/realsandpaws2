Meet the Breeds

Ten of the world's most-loved dogs, each with their own personality, quirks, and a whole lot of heart. Photos load fresh from the Dog CEO API, so every visit brings new furry faces.

Labrador Retriever

America's favorite for years running — outgoing, eager to please, and endlessly patient with kids and chaos alike.

Typical behaviors

  • Friendly
  • Energetic
  • Loyal

German Shepherd

Confident and brilliant working dogs, prized for their courage and devotion. They thrive with a job to do and a person to protect.

Typical behaviors

  • Intelligent
  • Protective
  • Trainable

Golden Retriever

Gentle, golden, and gloriously goofy. Their sunny temperament makes them wonderful family dogs and devoted companions.

Typical behaviors

  • Gentle
  • Affectionate
  • Playful

French Bulldog

Compact charmers with bat ears and big personalities. Low-key and adaptable, they're happiest curled up beside their humans.

Typical behaviors

  • Adaptable
  • Charming
  • Easygoing

Beagle

Merry, curious scent hounds led nose-first through life. Sociable and cheerful, they love company and a good adventure.

Typical behaviors

  • Curious
  • Merry
  • Sociable

Poodle

Famously clever and elegant, poodles come in three sizes. Behind the fancy coat is an athletic, eager-to-learn companion.

Typical behaviors

  • Smart
  • Elegant
  • Active

Pug

Wrinkly, comical, and full of love. Pugs were bred to be companions and take that job very seriously — usually from your lap.

Typical behaviors

  • Comical
  • Affectionate
  • Mellow

Siberian Husky

Striking sled dogs built for endurance and packed with energy. Friendly and free-spirited, they love to run and to "talk."

Typical behaviors

  • Energetic
  • Independent
  • Vocal

Dachshund

The lovable "wiener dog" — bold, clever, and bigger in spirit than in stature. Devoted to their people and surprisingly brave.

Typical behaviors

  • Bold
  • Clever
  • Devoted

Boxer

Playful, patient, and forever young at heart. Boxers are athletic family dogs who bond deeply and never quite outgrow the zoomies.

Typical behaviors

  • Playful
  • Loyal
  • Energetic
